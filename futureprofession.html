Future Profession

I'm Robbie Carlson, and my dream is to become a botanist — someone who studies plants,
ecosystems, and the incredible ways nature adapts and thrives. Ever since I was young, I've been
fascinated by the quiet brilliance of the natural world: the way a seed knows how to grow, how
flowers respond to light, and how entire forests communicate underground.

As a future botanist, I hope to explore the hidden life of plants — discovering new species,
protecting endangered ones, and contributing to research that helps preserve our planet's
biodiversity. Whether it's working in a greenhouse, hiking through a rainforest, or studying under a
microscope, I'm excited to dedicate my life to understanding and caring for the green world that
sustains us all.

Plants might not have voices, but they have stories — and I want to be one of the people who helps tell them.
